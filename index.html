About Tap Tap Shots

Tap to throw the ball into the air and score in Tap Tap Shots. A fun sports game where you don't have much time to score. Score as many points as possible.

Tap to score in Tap Tap Shots

You will control the basketball to get it into the basket as many times as possible. Each tap will push your ball. Your tap could make the ball go into the net or not. The height and position of the basket determine how many taps are needed. The player has to focus so they don't miss any shots. Tap Tap Shots is an endless game, so only you can limit this game.

Flexible net

There are two baskets on each side and you need to put the ball into these baskets in turns. With each throw, the height of the basket is always different. They can be higher or lower, or they can be moved from the highest position to the lowest position. Each time the ball is thrown into the basket, there will be a time limit. You are forced to adjust the ball into the basket within those few seconds. If you cannot get the ball into the basket, you will lose.

Tips to score more points

  • Because the time is limited, if you cannot adjust the distance properly, you should tap many times to get to the next turn faster.
  • Time your throws carefully and think quickly before the time runs out. Pay attention to the yellow bar running back to adjust in time.
  • You can get extra bonus points for clean throws that do not touch the rim. So timing and distance of the throw are very important.
  • To score more bonus points, you need to score several consecutive throws; the ball will heat up and burn. Pay attention to observe and learn from experience for the next dunk.

Please don't wait to join Tap Tap Shots. You can push yourself to get better every time by beating your old best and trying to move up the leaderboard. For sports fans, especially basketball fans, this is a fun training game. You'll be totally into the game and have the best times resting.
