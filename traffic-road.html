About Traffic Road

Traffic Road is a motorcycle racing game for speed enthusiasts only. Immerse yourself in the crowded urban roads and conquer all challenges.

How to play Traffic Road

Staying away from other cars on a busy highway is your main goal in this driving game. Skillful dodges and longer distances are both good ways to earn points, which reward accuracy and quick reactions.

Your mission may vary depending on the mode you choose.

  • Career: You have to overcome many different levels. There are many different missions to complete and get rewards.
  • Endless: Drive as far as you can. Try to compete for the highest score.
  • Time trial: Race against time
  • Free ride: You can enjoy your own ride. No need to worry about missions and time!

In each mode, players can choose from a variety of tracks and scenarios. Some tracks require a certain number of coins to unlock.

Improve your bike

Traffic Road is more than just about driving. It also lets you collect a huge number of bikes that you can then open and improve. You can fill your garage with your dream bikes by finishing fun missions and making as much in-game money as you can. There are 30 nice models to choose from.

Be smart about how you spend your money and either buy or improve the speed, handling, and brakes on your bike. Do more than just drive and race; work on making your bike run better.
